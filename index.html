ABOUT ICE CLEANING SOLUTIONS

Born from Legacy.
Driven by Vision.

Combining decades of industrial expertise with next-generation dry ice blasting technology.

ICE Cleaning Solutions was founded with a simple belief: that world-class cleaning technologies have the power to transform the way industries maintain, preserve, and enhance their assets.

Founded in Hyderabad in 2026, ICE Cleaning Solutions specializes in dry ice blasting services and dry ice manufacturing, serving the automotive and industrial sectors with innovative, environmentally responsible cleaning solutions.

While ICE Cleaning Solutions is a young company, it is built upon decades of industrial experience and entrepreneurial heritage through its parent organization, Atul Group of Companies, headquartered in Bhilai, Chhattisgarh.

For generations, the Atul Group has been a trusted name in Central India, operating across multiple industries including industrial gases, PCC pole manufacturing, cement pipe manufacturing, and automotive dry-cleaning services.

This strong foundation has enabled ICE Cleaning Solutions to combine the agility of a modern startup with the operational strength and technical expertise of an established industrial group.

PRODUCTS & SERVICES

Advanced Dry Ice Blasting Solutions

ICE Cleaning Solutions provides advanced dry ice blasting services and dry ice manufacturing solutions designed for businesses that demand cleaner operations, reduced downtime, and superior asset protection.

Using high-velocity dry ice pellets, our non-abrasive cleaning technology removes grease, oil, carbon deposits, paint residue, dust, and production build-up without damaging the underlying surface. Unlike conventional cleaning methods, dry ice blasting requires no water, leaves no secondary waste, and significantly reduces chemical dependency, making it a safer, cleaner, and more sustainable solution.

Our services are trusted by automotive workshops, luxury vehicle dealerships, manufacturing facilities, industrial plants, and maintenance teams seeking a faster and more effective approach to equipment and asset cleaning.
